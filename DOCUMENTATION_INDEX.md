## 🎊 Documentation Philosophy

Every doc has a purpose:
- **Guides** walk you through processes
- **References** provide quick lookups
- **Overviews** give big-picture understanding
- **Checklists** ensure nothing is missed
- **Summaries** confirm what was delivered

No fluff, no filler - just what you need to launch and use the system successfully.
